Ticker: SAF.PA

Company: Safran — aerospace propulsion and equipment with meaningful aftermarket exposure.

Thesis

  • Aftermarket economics: A large installed base + recurring spares/MRO can create durable, high-margin cash flows.
  • Long-duration demand: Commercial aviation growth and fleet utilization drive steady service revenue over time.
  • Quality compounder: If execution holds, the market can continue to reward consistency + cash generation.

What I think the market is missing

  • Where we are in the cycle vs normalized aftermarket profitability.
  • How quickly mix shifts toward higher-margin services as the installed base grows.

Catalysts / What to watch

  • Aftermarket growth and margin progression
  • Production cadence / delivery trends across key airframe platforms
  • Guidance updates and free cash flow conversion

Key risks

  • Aviation downturn or OEM delivery disruptions
  • Program-specific execution issues / cost inflation
  • FX and macro sensitivity
